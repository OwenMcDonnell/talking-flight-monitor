Takeoff assistant checklist
Pretakeoff checklist (partial assist mode):

Engines on.
Autothrottle on.
Autobreak RTO
Wing leveler on.
Pitot heat on.
Autopilot on.
Set vertical speed.
Autopilot altitude lock on (must be locked before setting altitude below).
Set autopilot altitude.
Set autopilot airspeed (faster than takeoff speed).
Turn off parking break.
Turn on toga or power engines to max power/thrust.
